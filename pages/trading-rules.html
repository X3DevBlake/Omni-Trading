Spot & Futures Trading Rules

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Trading Mechanisms

Omni utilizes a highly efficient matching engine evaluating orders on a strict Price-Time priority algorithm. Market Orders are executed immediately against the best available prices on the Order Book. Limit Orders rest on the book until the specific price threshold is met.

2. Price Limits & Collar Logic

To prevent drastic market manipulation, Omni enforces price deviation limits during periods of extreme volatility. Orders deviating beyond +/- 10% of the Mark Price on highly liquid pairs may face instant rejection to protect users from cascading liquidations.

3. Liquidation Parameters

USDT-M Futures utilize the Omni Global Mark Price mechanism to calculate unrealized PNL and margin levels. If a user's isolated or cross margin drops below the required Maintenance Margin Ratio, our robust liquidation engine incrementally unwinds positions.
